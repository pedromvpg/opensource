R&D Lab (for-profit) BTC Inc division. Builds, deploys, and maintains bitcoin-native technology across events, media, and corporate products. Staffed by existing BTC Inc engineers, no new hires.
Grant Program (501c3) Separate nonprofit. Funds external open source developers working on Bitcoin, Lightning, Nostr, and AI. Tax-deductible donations from corporate sponsors. Grantees are independent contributors, not employees.
