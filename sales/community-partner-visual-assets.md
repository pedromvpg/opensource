# Community Partner — Visual Assets Spec

Two assets a designer can build fast. Both should match the Open Source Hub look (see `sales/outreach-contacts.html`: near-black background, orange `#FF9500` accent, Geist / Geist Mono type). Keep them dark, technical, understated — no marketing gloss.

---

## Asset 1 — Community Partners Wall (on-site)

**Purpose:** one panel at the Hub entrance listing every partner community. The single highest-visibility, lowest-cost recognition item.

**Format**
- Large-format print (portrait or landscape banner) **or** a looping full-screen slide on a Hub display. Design once, output to both.
- Safe area: keep content off the outer 8–10% (print bleed / screen overscan).

**Layout**
- **Header:** "COMMUNITY PARTNERS" — Geist Mono, uppercase, letter-spaced, muted white. Small Open Source Hub lockup top-left.
- **Subhead (one line):** "The communities bringing their builders to the Open Source Hub."
- **Grid of partner tiles:** logo (or wordmark if no logo) on a subtle `rgba(255,255,255,0.04)` card with a hairline border — same card treatment as the app. Auto-size the grid to the count; **Anchor Partners** rendered larger / in the top row.
- **Footer:** orange hairline rule + "Bitcoin Asia 2026 · Open Source Hub · Hong Kong."

**Production notes**
- Collect logos as SVG/PNG at outreach time (add a "logo" ask to partner onboarding). Fallback: monospace wordmark on a card (the app already does this — reuse the initials/wordmark style).
- Build it to **reflow** — you won't know the final partner count until ~week 6, so use a grid that looks right at 8, 16, or 30 tiles.
- One editable master (Figma/SVG) so late additions are a 2-minute drop-in.

---

## Asset 2 — "Proud Community Partner" social graphic (pre-event)

**Purpose:** give partners something to *post* — their announcement to members becomes your reach. Co-brandable so it feels like theirs.

**Format**
- **Square 1080×1080** (IG/X primary) + a **1600×900** variant (X/LinkedIn/email header).

**Layout**
- Dark background, orange accent.
- **Badge/lockup:** "PROUD COMMUNITY PARTNER" arc or block over the Open Source Hub mark.
- **Fill-in slot:** big centered space for **[COMMUNITY NAME / LOGO]** — the one thing that changes per partner. Keep it a named layer so filling 20 of these takes minutes.
- **Standing line:** "Bringing our builders to the Open Source Hub · Bitcoin Asia 2026 · Hong Kong · late Aug."
- **Optional strip:** "Free developer passes for our members →" with room for a short link.

**Production notes**
- Deliver as a template with one swappable text/logo layer + an exportable batch, so you can generate a graphic per partner without a designer in the loop each time.
- Give partners **both sizes + a suggested caption** in the follow-up (pair it with the partner-facing blurb in `community-partner-recognition.md`).

## Nice-to-have (only if time allows)
- **Delegate badge ribbon artwork** — a small "[COMMUNITY] DELEGATE" ribbon/sticker template matching badge stock. Same swappable-name approach. This is the highest-emotion on-site item (see recognition spec) — worth doing if the badge vendor can accommodate.
- **Meetup-board header card** — a printed "COMMUNITY MEETUPS THIS WEEK" topper for the pin-board.

## Hand-off checklist
- [ ] Confirm brand assets (logo files, exact hex, font licenses) from the Hub design lead.
- [ ] Confirm wall dimensions / screen resolution with the build-out team.
- [ ] Confirm badge stock + ribbon feasibility with the badge vendor.
- [ ] Decide print vendor + deadline (partner list locks ~week 6).
