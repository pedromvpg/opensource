# Bitcoin 2026 — Open Source Hub
## Executive Report

**Conference:** Bitcoin Vegas 2026 — The Venetian, Las Vegas
**Dates:** April 27–29, 2026
**Track:** Open Source Hub & Open Source Stage

---

## Headline Metric

> **+68% increase in Open Source tickets** vs. Bitcoin 2025.

The Open Source Hub is no longer a side track — it is one of the fastest-growing programs of the conference. The expansion reflects a deliberate, multi-year strategy to put builders at the center of Bitcoin Vegas and bring the cypherpunk core of the ecosystem on-site.

---

## Strategic Thesis

The Open Source Hub turns Bitcoin Vegas into a working environment for the developers, designers, and protocol engineers shipping the network's future. By co-locating the Open Source Stage with project stations, workshops, and live Lightning/Nostr activations, we give the builder community three days of **intense experimentation, testing, and interoperation** — not a tradeshow booth, but a working lab.

This is the strategic value: proximity. Devs from different stacks (Lightning, Nostr, ecash, hardware, AI) end up in the same room, hands on each other's code, for 72 hours.

---

### 2. The Open Source Stage

Three days of programming, co-located with the Hub for maximum dev-to-dev proximity. Protocol engineers and core contributors present deep dives on:

- Protocol security, attacks and defenses
- Privacy improvements
- Payments and Lightning
- Ossification vs. evolution

**No marketing talks — just shipping code.**

---

### 3. Nostr Zaps Live with PubPay

Speakers earn sats in real-time via Lightning zaps powered by **[pubpay.me](https://pubpay.me/live)**. The audience votes with value, not applause. It's a working demonstration of value-for-value media, run live on the main Open Source Stage.

---

### 4. 101 Sessions — The Self-Custody Master Class

Foundational sessions hosted by two of the most trusted educators in Bitcoin, designed to take both beginners and experienced Bitcoiners deeper into self-custody.

- **[BTC Sessions](https://x.com/BTCsessions)** — World's most trusted Bitcoin-only educator since 2016. ([btcsessions.ca](https://www.btcsessions.ca/))
- **[Bitcoin Mentor](https://x.com/theBTCmentor)** — 1-on-1 mentorship and corporate Bitcoin education. ([bitcoinmentor.io](https://bitcoinmentor.io/))

Coverage spans wallets, keys, hardware, multisig, collaborative custody, inheritance, and node operation. From first principles to first sats — no prior knowledge required.

---

### 5. Workshops — Show, Tell, Build

The latest and greatest projects in Bitcoin in a **hands-on, participatory** format. Builders walk attendees through their tools live: install, configure, sign, broadcast — all in the room.

---

### 6. Dev Takeover

A self-initiated submission program open to anyone with a new or developing project. The biggest Bitcoin conference in the world gives the floor back to whoever has something worth showing. ([Submit a proposal](https://github.com/BTCMedia/Bitcoin2026-DEV-TAKEOVER))

---

### 7. BitDevs at Bitcoin Vegas

The iconic Bitcoin seminars come to the Open Source Stage. Socratic format: recent PRs, BIPs, implementation trade-offs. The highest-signal technical forum in Bitcoin, broadcast at conference scale. Participation expected.

---

### 8. Bitcoin Applied — Tech Running Throughout the Conference

The conference itself runs on the technology it celebrates. Three live applications across the floor:

- **Ecash Giveaway with Zeus** — Cashu ecash distributed to attendees in-app, demonstrating bearer-token Bitcoin at conference scale.
- **Signature Inscription with Stand with Crypto** — On-chain inscription as a record of attendee advocacy.
- **Nostr User Generation Capture** — On-boarding flow that issues attendees a Nostr identity at the door, plugging them into the open social graph from day one.

These aren't demos. They are the connective tissue of the conference experience.

## Why This Matters

The Open Source Hub is where the long-tail of Bitcoin development meets its largest audience. **68% YoY growth in dev tickets** is a leading indicator: the builder community is voting with their time. By bringing project stations, hardware, workshops, BitDevs, and live Lightning/Nostr activations under one roof — and by running the conference itself on the same stack — Bitcoin Vegas 2026 positions itself as the working capital of open-source Bitcoin for the year.
