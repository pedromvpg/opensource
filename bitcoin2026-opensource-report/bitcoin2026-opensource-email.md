# Open Source Hub — Bitcoin 2026

### The Hub

3 days of intense experimentation, testing, and learning. The Hub leveraged proximity with the Open Source Stage to bring developers together and build, experiment, and interoperate.

### Project Stations

Some of the most relevant projects in Bitcoin, on the floor:

- **[Zeus](https://zeusln.com/)** — Lightning + ecash wallet and infrastructure
- **[ProductionReady](https://productionready.org/)** — Bitcoin node implementation focused on monetary use
- **Free Samourai + Roman Sterlingov** — Awareness campaign for Bitcoin prisoners ([billandkeonne.org](https://billandkeonne.org/) · [freeroman.org](https://freeroman.org/))
- **Freemarket** — [Shopstr](https://shopstr.store/), [Milk Market](https://milk.market/), [Naughty Goat](https://naughtygoat.co/)
- **[SeedSigner](https://seedsigner.com/)** — DIY hardware wallet
- **[bitcoin++](https://btcpp.dev/)** — Developer-focused conference series; long-form lectures and workshops
- **[Satsigner](https://github.com/satsigner/satsigner)** — Privacy-first Bitcoin mobile wallet/signer (Lightning, Ark, ecash, Nostr on roadmap)
- **[Chain Duel](https://chainduel.net/)** — Lightning- and Nostr-enabled multiplayer game
- **Nostr Stream** — Stage and lounge interviews live on Nostr with [ReverendHodl](https://x.com/ReverendHodl)

### Nostr Zaps Live

Speakers zapped live on the Open Source Stage with [pubpay.me](https://pubpay.me/live).

### 101 Sessions

Self-custody master class for beginners and experienced bitcoiners with [BTC Sessions](https://x.com/BTCsessions) and [Bitcoin Mentor](https://x.com/theBTCmentor).

### Workshops

The latest and greatest projects in Bitcoin — show and tell, hands-on, participatory.

### Dev Takeover

Self-initiated submissions, open to anyone with a new or developing project.
