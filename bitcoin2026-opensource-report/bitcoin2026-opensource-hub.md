## Overview

BTC Inc, the industry pioneer in Bitcoin news and organizer of the world's largest Bitcoin conference, today unveiled its newly expanded Open Source Hub for Bitcoin 2026, slated for **April 27–29** at the **Venetian Conference Center in Las Vegas**. Building on a proven track record of success, this dedicated expansion underscores the event's unwavering commitment to fueling collaboration, exploration, and breakthrough innovation within Bitcoin's open-source ecosystem.

The Open Source Stage remains a cornerstone of the event, hosting in-depth discussions with Bitcoin Core developers, Lightning engineers, and privacy researchers. This year's enhancements include new interactive features and dedicated spaces for collaboration, ensuring that Bitcoin 2026 continues to be a hub for advancing decentralized technology.

## What's New This Year

This newly expanded developer track underscores the pivotal role of open-source software in powering Bitcoin, enabling censorship-resistant and borderless financial tools. In addition to cutting-edge infrastructure talks on the Open Source Stage, this year's highlights include:

### Programming & Events

- **Hackathon Awards** — Innovative projects from Bitcoin++ hackathons will be celebrated live on stage.
- **BitDevs Special Event** — A deep-dive technical forum for those interested in Bitcoin research, protocol development, and security.
- **101 Content with [BTC Sessions](https://www.btcsessions.ca/?ref=b26-opensourcehub)**
- **Dev Takeover** — Open call for you to present your emerging tech. [Submit here](https://github.com/BTCMedia/Bitcoin2026-DEV-TAKEOVER)
- **Project Stations** — Offering developers a chance to showcase live demos of working Bitcoin tools.

### Interactive Experiences

- **Pubpay Live** — [pubpay.me/live](https://pubpay.me/live): Interact with speakers on stage by zapping them live and be part of the show.
- **Chain Duel Championship** — [chainduel.net](https://chainduel.net): An interactive gaming experience where participants compete to build the longest chain and win sats on the Lightning Network.
