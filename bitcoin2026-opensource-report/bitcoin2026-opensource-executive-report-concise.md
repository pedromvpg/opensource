# Bitcoin 2026 — Open Source Hub

**Las Vegas · April 27–29, 2026 · The Venetian**

---

## Headline

**+68% YoY growth in Open Source tickets.** The Hub is now one of the fastest-growing programs at Bitcoin Vegas.

---

## The Thesis

Three days of intense experimentation, testing, and interoperation — co-located with the Open Source Stage. Devs from Lightning, Nostr, ecash, hardware, and AI in the same room, hands on each other's code.

## Programming

- **Open Source Stage** — Protocol engineers and core contributors. Security, privacy, payments. No marketing talks.
- **Nostr Zaps Live** — Speakers earn sats in real-time via [pubpay.me](https://pubpay.me/live). Audience votes with value.
- **101 Sessions** — Self-custody master class with [BTC Sessions](https://x.com/BTCsessions) and [Bitcoin Mentor](https://x.com/theBTCmentor). Beginners to advanced.
- **Workshops** — The latest projects in Bitcoin, hands-on and participatory.
- **Dev Takeover** — Open submission for anyone with a new or developing project. [Submit](https://github.com/BTCMedia/Bitcoin2026-DEV-TAKEOVER).
- **BitDevs** — The iconic Bitcoin seminars. Highest signal in the room.
